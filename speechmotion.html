Use voice command

Say "ground" to add a ground to the scene.
Say "remove" to remove that ground.
Say "rotate" to enable rotation mode for Leap Motion gestures.
Say "stop rotate" to disable rotation mode for Leap Motion gestures.

Use Leap Motion

Move your index finger to move the box around.
In Rotation mode: Rotate your extended hand to rotate the box. The degree of rotation correlates to the rotation of your hand.
